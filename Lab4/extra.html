Lab 4 - Field of Spikes Extended

1. Extending Field of Spikes

  1. If you have not completed last week's advanced task, start by completing it before moving to the next task.

  2. Add UI elements in your Field of Spikes project to display the following information:

    • The x, y, and z coordinates of the plane, listed as three separate lines and aligned to the left, at the bottom of your widnow.
    • A blinking message asking the user to turn back if the user flies of the floor, positioned at the center of the window.
    • Whether the player can shoot a ball or not, at the top left corner of the window.
    • The total number of spikes at the start of the game and the number of spikes shot by the player (use format "Score:[shot]/[total] e.g. Sciore: 2/10), centered at the top of the window.
